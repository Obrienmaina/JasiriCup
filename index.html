About JasiriCup

JasiriCup is an initiative dedicated to empowering girls in rural and underserved Arid and Semi-Arid Land (ASAL) regions by addressing the critical gap in menstrual health and education. Many girls in these areas face limited access to affordable and sustainable menstrual products, leading to school absenteeism, stigma, and poor hygiene practices. JasiriCup provides reusable menstrual cups alongside culturally sensitive education to promote dignity, confidence, and long-term health. By focusing on these marginalized communities, the initiative aims to break taboos, support school retention, and foster gender equality through informed and sustainable menstrual care solutions.
